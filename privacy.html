Privacy Policy UK GDPR

Effective date: 11 Nov 2025 • App: SwipeSound • Bundle ID: uk.co.swipesound.SwipeSound

Who we are

SwipeSound Ltd (or individual developer) (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the SwipeSound iOS/iPadOS app and this website. We are based in the United Kingdom.

Contact: support@swipesound.co.uk

What we collect

Account & authentication

  • Spotify OAuth (PKCE): we receive an access token and (when provided by Spotify) a refresh token after you consent. We do not receive your Spotify password.
  • Tokens are stored securely on-device (Keychain). We do not persist your tokens on our servers unless you explicitly opt-in to cloud backup/sync (disabled by default).

Usage & diagnostics

  • Firebase Analytics: anonymous usage metrics (screens, events) to improve UX.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: crash reports & stack traces to fix stability issues.

Advertising

  • Google AdMob: shows ads. With your consent, AdMob may use device identifiers to personalize or measure ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in-app.

How we use data

  • To authenticate with Spotify and manage your playlists as requested (create, edit, add tracks).
  • To provide core app features (swipe UI, filters, recommendations).
  • To improve stability and performance (analytics & crash reports).
  • To fund the service via advertising and in-app purchases.

Legal basis (UK GDPR)

  • Performance of a contract: providing requested app features with your Spotify account.
  • Legitimate interests: ensuring app security, stability, and service improvement.
  • Consent: personalized ads & analytics where required. You can withdraw consent at any time in Settings.

Sharing & third parties

  • Spotify (APIs for authentication and playlist operations).
  • Google AdMob (ads; see Google partner sites policy).
  • Firebase (Analytics/Crashlytics; see privacy & security).
  • We do not sell personal data.

Data retention

  • Spotify tokens are stored in Keychain until you log out or revoke access in the app or Spotify settings.
  • Analytics/crash logs follow providers’ default retention (typically 2–14 months).

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you may have the right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict processing of your personal data, and to object or request portability. To exercise these rights, contact support@swipesound.co.uk.

International transfers

We and our processors (Spotify, Google, Firebase) may process data outside the UK. Where applicable, appropriate safeguards (e.g., SCCs) are relied upon by those processors.

Children

SwipeSound is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided us data, contact us to delete it.

Security

  • On-device secure storage (Keychain) for tokens.
  • Network requests over HTTPS.
  • Least-privilege scopes and revocation on logout.

Consent & choices

  • You can revoke Spotify access anytime in the app or in your Spotify account settings.
  • Toggle analytics/ad personalization in Settings.
  • Request data deletion via support@swipesound.co.uk.

Changes

We may update this policy. We will post changes here and update the effective date.
